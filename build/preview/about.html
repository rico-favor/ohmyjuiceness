our story

Our founder, passionate about healthier living, traveled around the world in search of manufacturers who shared the same vision. After visiting several overseas facilities, they made it their mission to bring quality, affordable, no-sugar orange juice to the Philippines.

our brand

oh my juiceness started with a simple mission:

To make fresh, healthy juice accessible in Manila — without added sugar, the fuss, or the preservatives.

In a tropical country full of soft drinks and sugar-packed options, we believe there should be something better. Something real. Something refreshing. Something pure.
