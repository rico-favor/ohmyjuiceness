Our Story

From One Machine to a Fresh Fruit Movement

OMJ started with a simple question: why is it so hard to get a genuinely fresh cup of orange juice? One machine later, the answer was rolling into malls across Metro Manila.

Every cup is squeezed on the spot from whole oranges you can see in the machine. No water, no sugar, no concentrates — just fruit.
