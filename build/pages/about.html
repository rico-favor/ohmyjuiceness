Our Story

From One Machine to a Fresh Fruit Movement

Oh My Juiceness started with a simple mission: make fresh, healthy juice accessible in Manila — without added sugar, the fuss, or the preservatives. What began as one vending machine has grown into multiple locations across Metro Manila, squeezing over half a million oranges and counting.

Why OMJ

  • 🍊 Real oranges — nothing from concentrate
  • 🚫 Nothing artificial — no sugar, no preservatives
  • ⚡ Freshly squeezed in under a minute
