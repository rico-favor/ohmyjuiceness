## Framing Guidance (16:9 → 9:16 crop-safe)

The source image is landscape 4:3. When composing the 16:9 video:

- **Centre the juicer mechanism** horizontally — the squeeze area and juice stream should sit in the centre third.
- **Leave ~20% headroom** above and below the machine so the 9:16 vertical crop lands cleanly on the juicer chamber and cup.
- Avoid placing key detail (oranges, juice stream, branding) in the outer 20% of the left/right edges.

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Use the highest-quality source image** — the 5712×4284 source has plenty of resolution for Veo's reference frame.
2. **Keep motion minimal** — subtle dolly or static camera sells "premium" better than dramatic moves.
3. **Emphasise the juice stream** — that's the hero moment: liquid gold pouring into the cup.
4. **Avoid people** — the machine is the star. Any human presence breaks the product-focus.
5. **Regenerate 3–4 times** — Veo output varies; pick the smoothest loop with the best juice-pour moment.
6. **Poster frame** — `hero-poster.jpg` (1600px) is the fallback still for browsers that can't autoplay video or on slow connections.
